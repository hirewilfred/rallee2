Our Story

We thought the fastest-growing sport deserved a finer wardrobe.

Rallee started courtside in Ontario, between rallies, with a simple frustration: the gear didn't match the game. So we built a label that treats pickleball like the lifestyle it has become — premium fabrics, a sun-washed palette, and a fit you'd happily wear to brunch after the third set.
